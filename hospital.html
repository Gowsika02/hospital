About Harmony Care Hospital

History: Founded in 1995, Harmony Care Hospital has served Wellness City with dedication and innovation for over 25 years. What began as a small clinic has grown into a multi-specialty healthcare facility known for patient-centered care and state-of-the-art treatment.

Vision: To be the most trusted healthcare partner for our community, ensuring a healthier tomorrow for all.

Mission: We are committed to delivering compassionate care, advancing medical knowledge, and fostering health and healing in every life we touch.

Accreditation: Harmony Care Hospital is accredited by the National Health Assurance Board (NHAB) and complies with international standards for patient safety and clinical excellence. (Dummy accreditation)
